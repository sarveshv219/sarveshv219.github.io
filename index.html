I am an M.S. student in ECE (Machine Learning) at USC, broadly interested in robot learning — particularly teaching robots to move and manipulate in the real world. I currently do research at the Dynamic Robotics and Control Laboratory with Prof. Quan Nguyen. Before USC, I spent two years at IISc Bengaluru working on legged robots and multi-robot systems, and interned at IIT Madras on service robotics.
